SUMMARY: Who are America's Got Talent (AGT) winners? Transforming data on AGT winners into a functional concept allows NBC to take advantage of research and increase viewer interest at the same time. Potential contestants can check their chance ofwinning against AGTâ€™s history and viewers can see if their next vote will change the trends.

PROJECT INSTRUCTIONS

NBC has hired your team to help them understand what AGT fans like about the winners of their show. Are winners selected purely on talent, or are there other factors at play? Are contestants more likely to win if they have a particular talent? NBC wants to better understand what the makeup of an AGT champion is so that they can use that information to guide their 2019 audition selection process.

METHOD

Figure 1 shows the steps my team completed to accomplish our objectives. Each chapter of the deliverable goes into detail regarding the different phases of the project. The final page details the steps required to complete the next phase of the project.

Before creating the deliverable, I referred to Chapter 8: Deliverable Basics in Communicating Design by Dan Brown. I included the key elements he recommends as shown in Figure 2. Consistency, alignment, content hierarchy, and legibility are incorporated to create a usable document that conveys the overall purpose of the project, including the key questions and issues that need to be addressed for the project to move forward.

THINGS I WOULD DO DIFFERENTLY

Several of the pages featured a dark gray background with white text. While this design was included to visually break up the document, I received feedback that repeating this design gives the impression that the pages are related or indicate a new section of the document. Rather than repeating the same design, I would use multiple variations to keep the design of the document fresh.
